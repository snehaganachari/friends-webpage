FRIENDS

About

Friends" is a popular American television sitcom that aired on NBC from September 22, 1994, to May 6, 2004. Created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, the show revolves around a group of six friends living in Manhattan, New York City. This show is filled with laughter, heartwarming moments, and unforgettable bonds.
